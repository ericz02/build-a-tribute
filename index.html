Here's a time line of LeBron's life:

  • 1984 - Born in Akron, Ohio
  • 2003 - Drafted to the Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2010 - Left the Cavaliers to join the Miami Heat
  • 2014 - Returned to the Cavaliers
  • 2018 - Joined the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2020 - Won his 4th NBA Championship
